Tier 2: The plateau
10 to 20k/mo. Has the shape of a business. None of the leverage. Chris fits the archetype cleanly — 50 active clients, sister doing outreach, founder still in DMs, sales, and delivery.
where he is now
  • ~50 active clients, revenue flatlining
  • Sister Jasmine doing 200 outbound DMs/day, £500/mo, outreach only — no full setter
  • No coach. Chris delivers all clients personally
  • Chris running: coaching, sales calls, DMs, ops
  • Has consumed a lot of training, lacks clarity on execution
the problem
  • Sales close rate ~30%, Chris uncomfortable with sales
  • DM system not tracked — no response rate, no KPIs, no consequences
  • Ads = boosted posts only, no system, no creative testing
  • Cannot fill a coach predictably — capped at his own delivery time
  • Under-tracks every department, fulfilment ceiling at 50 clients
the fix
  • Rebuild DM system + progress Jasmine into full setter (track msgs, replies, offers, booked, showed, closed)
  • Ad strategy: 4 structured boosted-post ads, £15/day → £100/day
  • Transformational sales: fear/objection/belief handling → close rate 50-60%
  • Systemize coach hiring — first delivery coach so Chris stops being the ceiling
  • Department-wide tracking + weekly review with Sam
next level (T2.5 the crossover)
  • 20 to 30k consistently
  • First delivery coach filled and profitable
  • Chris fully out of DMs and sales
  • Jasmine running setter function autonomously
  • Real KPI rhythm — ads → DMs → calls → close → delivery, all measured weekly
founder pathway
Burnt-out coach trying to do everything. Identity: "I do, therefore the business runs." Goal: hand off DMs, sales, and delivery in that priority order. The shift is I do, to I oversee. Specific resistance flagged: Chris believes operational tracking is hard "because of how his brain works" — delegate tracking to Jasmine early, do not put it on him.
