Cooking tiles get a rotating green ring around the glyph so "this is the one running" is unmistakable. Click any mini-tile → popover anchors to its right edge with the full card (status, inline permission row, focus button). Click outside the popover or hit Esc to close.

3 · Decisions baked in

  • 12 cards is the design baseline, not a hard cap. Grid is auto-fit minmax(430px, 1fr) so >12 wraps cleanly with vertical scroll.
  • Mini-tile labels show only on hover. Color rim + glyph carry the at-a-glance signal. Names below would clutter and force horizontal scroll.
  • Cooking gets the rotating ring, not just a status word. It's the one state where "currently running" matters most for routing attention — it earns the animation budget.
  • ASCII fallbacks render at 45% opacity to read as background noise, not as content. Cards never look broken; they just look quiet.
  • Popover anchors right-of-strip, never overlaps the strip itself. So you can scan the strip while a popover is open.
