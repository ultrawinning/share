Meeting Purpose

Onboard Christie to a new nutrition and fitness program.

Key Takeaways
  • Goal: Reach 115–118 lbs (from 125.6 lbs) with 18–19% body fat while training for a pre-Thanksgiving marathon.
  • Plan: A 4-phase program starting with a 60-day caloric deficit (1400 kcal) to lean out, followed by marathon fueling, post-race recovery, and long-term maintenance.
  • Focus: Prioritize protein (140g/day) to build and protect muscle, which is critical for metabolism, mobility, and injury prevention, especially for a runner.
  • Actionable: Begin the 14-day detox immediately to reduce inflammation and stabilize energy. Start tracking all food in MyFitnessPal to ensure adherence to the 1400 kcal target.
Topics
Goals & Baseline
  • Baseline (from DEXA): 125.6 lbs, 19.9% body fat.
  • Weight Goal: 115–118 lbs, achieved sustainably and with high energy (unlike a previous carnivore diet that caused fatigue).
  • Body Fat Goal: 18–19%.
  • Marathon: Scheduled for the week before Thanksgiving.
  • Other Goals: Improve cardiovascular health, build endurance, establish a sustainable nutrition plan, and fix gut issues.
4-Phase Program
  • Phase 1: Caloric Deficit (First 60 Days)
  • Purpose: Reduce adipose tissue to create a lighter frame for marathon training.
  • Nutrition: 1400 kcal/day (40% protein, 30% carbs, 30% fat).
  • Workouts: 3x/week strength, 2x/week Zone 2 cardio, 1x/week run.
  • Phase 2: Marathon Readiness
  • Purpose: Fuel peak performance and protect muscle.
  • Nutrition: Increase to 1800–1900 kcal/day with a carb load.
  • Workouts: Shift focus to running (3–4x/week); reduce strength to 2x/week.
  • Phase 3: Post-Marathon Recovery
  • Purpose: Return to metabolic health and continue body recomposition.
  • Nutrition: Revert to 1400 kcal/day.
  • Phase 4: Maintenance & Identity Shift
  • Purpose: Establish a sustainable lifestyle and identity.
  • Nutrition: Find a long-term maintenance plan.
Nutrition Strategy (Phase 1)
  • Calorie Target: 1400 kcal/day.
  • Macro Split: 40% protein (140g), 30% carbs (105g), 30% fat (47g).
  • Rationale: The high protein target is critical for muscle growth and protection. Ana provided strategies to reach it, such as adding protein powder to coffee ("proffy") to stabilize energy and avoid cortisol spikes from caffeine on an empty stomach.
  • 14-Day Detox: Start immediately to reduce inflammation and reset taste buds.
  • Meal Timing: Eat breakfast within 30–45 mins of waking. Consume a carb snack before midday workouts to protect muscle. Eat protein and carbs within 30–60 mins post-workout.
Workout Strategy (Phase 1)
  • Strength Training (3x/week): Full-body superset workouts (37–40 mins).
  • Method: Progressive overload, starting with 5 challenging reps.
  • Schedule: Mon/Wed/Fri (flexible).
  • Zone 2 Cardio (2x/week): Low-intensity activity (e.g., fast walking) to accelerate fat loss without increasing cortisol.
  • Schedule: Tue/Thu.
  • Running (1x/week): Free-form run for marathon prep.
  • Schedule: Sat.
  • July Challenge: 10 mins/day jump rope for bone density.
Supplements
  • Current: Creatine, Magnesium, L-Theanine.
  • New Recommendations:
  • Omega-3 (Thorn): For inflammation reduction.
  • L-Glutamine: To help with gut discomfort and bloating.
Tracking & Accountability
  • Daily: Weigh-in, log all food in MyFitnessPal.
  • Weekly: Measure waist and hips (e.g., Mondays).
  • Monthly: Take progress photos (front, back, side).
  • Coach Check-ins: Ana will review data every 2–3 days.
  • Calls: A follow-up call in 2 weeks, then every 3 weeks, with a 60-day assessment to review Phase 1 progress.
