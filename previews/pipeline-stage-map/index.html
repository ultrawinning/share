Pipeline Stage Map SWIMLANE DRAFT

The 5 master milestones run across the top (the funnel HPFHQ counts). Each board has its own lane; its sub-stages sit under the milestone they map to. Read down a column to see everything that means e.g. "Booked" across all boards; read across a lane to see one board's journey. Duplicate labels are consolidated to one card (the "folds:" line shows what merged). Dashed cards are outcomes (cancelled / no-show / lost) — what happened, recorded at the milestone reached, never a step backward.

Where do losses live? There's deliberately no "Lost" milestone — a loss is an outcome (the dashed cards), recorded at the milestone the lead actually reached, so you can always ask "of the leads that booked, how many did we lose and why." Adding a stage = add it to the map under one of the five milestones (in code — no self-serve editor); a guard makes sure every live stage is mapped.
