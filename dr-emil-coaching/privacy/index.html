Privacy Policy

Dr Emil Coaching · Effective 1 June 2026

This policy explains how Dr Emil Coaching (operated by Emil Hodzovic, the "we"/"us") handles data accessed through the Oura API when you connect your Oura account to support your 1:1 coaching. Questions: coaching@doctoremil.com.

What we access

Only with your explicit consent, granted through Oura's standard OAuth authorization screen, we access your Oura data on a read-only basis. Depending on the scopes you approve, this may include sleep, readiness and activity summaries, heart rate, workouts, SpO2, and basic profile details (such as age, height and weight). You choose which scopes to grant, and you can decline any of them.

How we use it

We use this data for one purpose only: to support your personal health and fitness coaching with Emil. We do not use it for advertising, profiling, or any automated decision-making.

What we cannot do

The integration is read-only. It cannot modify, delete, or post anything to your Oura account, and it has no access to your Oura login credentials or billing.

Storage & security

Access tokens are stored in an encrypted secrets manager, never in plain code or shared documents. Data pulled for your coaching is kept in private records accessible only to Emil.

Sharing

We never sell your data and never share it with third parties. It is seen only by Emil for the purpose of coaching you.

Retention & deletion

We retain data only for the duration of your coaching relationship. You can request deletion of your stored data at any time by emailing coaching@doctoremil.com, and we will delete it promptly.

Revoking access

You can revoke this integration's access at any time from your Oura account under connected applications. Access stops immediately when you do.

Not medical advice

Coaching provided using this data is for general health and fitness purposes and is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time via coaching@doctoremil.com.

Changes

We may update this policy; the effective date above reflects the latest version.
