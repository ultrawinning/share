Setter Board — what's new

A quick brief to walk the setters through. Two small additions + a refresher on the flow. Nothing you do day-to-day changes — this just makes the board clearer.

🩶 1. "No email" leads now look dimmed

Some IG / ManyChat DM leads come in with only a name and the ManyChat link — no email, no phone yet. These are placeholders. They now show up greyed out with a small no email tag.

Nothing is wrong with them — they just haven't given us an email yet. Work them normally. The tag disappears the moment an email lands on the card (from the DM flow, a re-paste of the ManyChat link, or you adding it).

♻️ 2. A "Retire" hint when a placeholder books

When one of those email-less placeholder leads books a call, the system notices and shows a yellow strip on the old placeholder card:

📅 Booked as [Name] — looks like this stub converted. Retire

That means: "this placeholder looks like the same person who just booked." The booked lead is the real, live one to work. Click Retire to clear the old placeholder so the board stays clean and you don't work the same person twice.

It's safe: Retire isn't a disqualify — it doesn't count against your DQ numbers, and it's reversible if you ever retire the wrong one.

Do retire when the name matches and you recognise it's the same person who booked.
Don't retire if you're unsure, or if you're still actively working that placeholder — just leave it. Only real look-alikes are ever flagged, but your judgement wins.

🗂️ 3. Quick refresher — how the columns flow

Left → right is the lead's journey. Drag a card to move it:

  • New Leads — unclaimed. Grab one → drag to Warm or Hot to start working it.
  • Warm — claimed, first outreach done, waiting for a reply.
  • Hot — they're replying. Active conversation — your daily focus.
  • Call Proposed — you've pitched the call, waiting on a yes/no.
  • Link Sent — booking link sent. Nudge if they don't book in ~10–15 min.
  • Call Booked — booked. The dialer takes it from here.

The "no email" tag and the Retire hint (above) only ever appear in the working columns (Call Proposed / Link Sent) — where a lead is close to booking.
