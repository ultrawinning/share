Country Qualification Tiers

How the Typeform booking flow qualifies leads by location. Country is one of four qualification criteria (alongside occupation, income willingness, and timeline).

TIER 1 — GREEN

Auto-Qualified (4 countries)

TIER 3 — DQ (DISQUALIFIED)

All Other Countries

Any country not listed above. In the Typeform, this is the "Other" option in the country dropdown.
Leads from unlisted countries are disqualified on location. Combined with other criteria failures, this typically results in a Red (DQ) outcome. DQ leads are routed to the Lean Body 90 landing page (dango.co/lean-body-90).
How the full qualification works:
  • 4 criteria scored: Occupation, Location (country), Income willingness, Timeline
  • Green: All 4 pass → fully qualified → routed to book a call
  • Amber: 3 of 4 pass → review required (setter follows up)
  • Red (DQ): Income unwilling, OR occupation = "other", OR timeline = "researching", OR ≤1 criteria pass

What this means for Tier 2 countries: A lead from Germany who is a business owner, willing to invest, and ready ASAP will be Amber (3/4 pass, location fails). The setter can still book them — they just don't auto-qualify.
